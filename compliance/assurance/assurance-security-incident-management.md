## <a name="response-management-process"></a><span data-ttu-id="85335-139">回應管理程式</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85335-139">Response management process</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="85335-140">Microsoft 線上服務安全性小組和服務小組共同運作，並對以 NIST 800-61 回應管理階段為基礎的安全性事件採取相同的做法：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85335-140">Microsoft online services security teams and service teams work together on and take the same approach to security incidents, which is based on the NIST 800-61 response management phases:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="85335-141">**準備**：指能夠進行回應的組織準備工作，包括工具、處理常式、稱職狀況和準備就緒。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85335-141">**Preparation**: Refers to the organizational preparation that is needed to be able to respond, including tools, processes, competencies, and readiness.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="85335-142">**偵測 & 分析**：指的是在實際執行環境中偵測安全性事件的活動，並分析所有事件，以確認安全性事件的真偽。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85335-142">**Detection & analysis**: Refers to the activity to detect a security incident in a production environment and to analyze all events to confirm the authenticity of the security incident.</span></span>
