## <a name="ferpa-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="5368f-105">FERPA 概述</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5368f-105">FERPA overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5368f-106">「家庭教育權力」和隱私權法案 (FERPA) 是美國聯邦法律，可保護學生教育記錄的隱私權，包含個人身分識別及目錄資訊。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5368f-106">The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) is a US federal law that protects the privacy of students' education records, including personally identifiable and directory information.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5368f-107">FERPA 已頒佈，以確保父母和學生年齡18歲及更舊的版本可以存取這些記錄、要求變更，以及控制資訊的洩漏，但在特定和有限的情況下，FERPA 允許披露，但不同意。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5368f-107">FERPA was enacted to ensure that parents and students age 18 and older can access those records, request changes to them, and control the disclosure of information, except in specific and limited cases where FERPA allows for disclosure without consent.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="5368f-108">法律適用于學校、學校地區和任何其他接受美國教育部門之資助的機構，也就是幾乎所有的上市公司、12學校和學校地區，也就是公開及私人的所有次要機構。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5368f-108">The law applies to schools, school districts, and any other institution that receives funding from the US Department of Education — that is, virtually all public K-12 schools and school districts, as well as most post-secondary institutions, both public and private.</span></span>
